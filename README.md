# Ride-Sharing_Matching_VS_Bruce_Force_Greedy_Matching_Maximum_Flow_Peguins_Search_Optimaztion
Team Members: Idris King, Andrew Walldorf, Jacob Russell, Jason Brown

Problem: As cities grow, so does the need for good coordination and efficient transportation. To move a significant amount of people every day, new algorithms continue to be explored and written to find the most efficient method to move the most amount of people while also keeping costs and wait times down. Not only do these algorithms help find the best way to do this task, but they can also extend to other real-world applications such as supply chain logistics and delivery pathing.

The Algorithms we used are Brute force, Greedy Matching, Maxiumum flow, and Penguins Search Optimization.
